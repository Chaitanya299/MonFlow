CRDT P2P Sync Architecture

Ensuring "Extreme Privacy" for global bill splitting

E2EE P2P Sync Flow
Transport

User A and User B exchange Encrypted CRDT Operations through a "Blind" Relay.

[User A] --(AES-256)--> [Relay] --(AES-256)--> [User B]
   ^                                              |
   +---------------(CRDT Merge)------------------+
Privacy Guarantee

The Relay never sees the content (debts, names, amounts). It only sees encrypted noise.
